Game Rules

  1. The game is broken up into three rounds.
  2. The first two rounds allow the player to bank and earn money.
  3. The last round is a sudden death wrong that will determine if he or she will walk away with the total money banked in the two previous rounds.
  4. An incorrect answer breaks the chain and loses all the money.
  5. The player press a bank button, the accumulated money is stored, and the “Money Tree” resets to zero.
